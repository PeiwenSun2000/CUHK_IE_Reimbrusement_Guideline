# CUHK Department of Information Engineering · RPg Overseas Conference Reimbursement Guide

> This guide is for IE research postgraduate (RPg) students applying for and claiming reimbursement related to overseas academic conferences.

> **Important: Confirm whether you will attend in person**
>
> - **Attending in person:** Prepare `material_before/` and **submit printed copies to Connie** before the trip (Conference Grant application, etc.); after the conference, prepare `material_after/` and **email to Fandy** for reimbursement.
> - **Not attending in person** (e.g., proceedings-only, virtual registration only): **Do not submit `material_before/`**; keep all payment records and, after the conference, prepare `material_after/` and **email to Fandy** only.

| Folder | Submit to | When required |
|--------|-----------|---------------|
| `material_before/` | **Printed copies to Connie** | Only if **attending in person** |
| `material_after/` | **Email to Fandy** | After the conference (regardless of attendance) |

## Process Overview

Reimbursement for an overseas conference typically involves **three layers** of application and approval:

| Stage | Office | Purpose |
|-------|--------|---------|
| 1. Graduate School Conference Grant | Graduate School | Fund registration, airfare, etc. |
| 2. IE Seminar Presentation | Department of IE | Mandatory pre-trip mock presentation |
| 3. Post-trip reimbursement | Graduate School + IE | Submit payment proofs to claim expenses |

## Pre-Trip Materials (in-person attendance only)

Example folder: `material_before/`  
**Submit to:** **Connie**  
**Submission method:** Printed copies  
**When required:** Only if you **attend in person**; if not, skip this section and go to [Post-Trip Materials](#post-trip-materials-required-for-all-reimbursement).  
**Purpose:** Attach when submitting the Conference Grant application. Numeric prefixes indicate suggested ordering.

| File | Type | Description | Checklist item |
|------|------|-------------|----------------|
| `0_Pricing.pdf` | Web screenshot | Conference registration pricing page: student fee, dates, host city, etc. | Conference info / registration fee basis |
| `2_flight_quota_1.jpg.pdf` | Screenshot | **Airfare quotation 1** (one economy round-trip option; quotation should be **higher than** the actual reimbursement amount) | Checklist #2 (quotation 1 of 2) |
| `2_flight_quota_2.jpg.pdf` | Screenshot | **Airfare quotation 2** (another economy round-trip option; quotation should be **higher than** the actual reimbursement amount) | Checklist #2 (quotation 2 of 2) |
| `3_flight_iternery.pdf` | Text PDF | Booked itinerary: flight numbers, dates, e-ticket number | Transport details |
| `3_flight_reciept.pdf` | Text PDF | Booking platform flight receipt (e-ticket number and amount) | Checklist #3 |
| `3_flight_payment_record.pdf` | Screenshot | Credit card app charge record for the flight | Checklist #3 |
| `5_conference_pamphlet.pdf` | Screenshot | Conference homepage: name, dates, location | Checklist #5 |
| `6_registration-receipt.pdf` | Text PDF | Official conference registration receipt | Checklist #6 |
| `7_abstract.pdf` | Text PDF | Abstract of the accepted paper | Checklist #7 |
| `8_Decision.pdf` | Screenshot | **Paper acceptance notification email** | Checklist #8 |
| `9_Improving Postgraduate Learning.pdf` | Text PDF | CLEAR **IPL attendance/completion record** (Presentation Skills, Communication Skills, etc.) | Checklist #9 |

> **Note:** Image-based PDFs (`.jpg.pdf` or screenshots) may not allow text copy. Keep screenshots clear with visible dates, amounts, and flight details. For `2_flight_quota_*`, **quotations** are enough at application time—you do not need to have purchased tickets yet. If tickets are purchased early, also attach `3_flight_*` documents (not listed in the example folder structure; add as needed).

## Post-Trip Materials (required for all reimbursement)

Example folder: `material_after/`  
**Submit to:** **Fandy**  
**Submission method:** Email  
**When required:** Reimbursement is processed after the conference, whether or not you attended in person.  
**Purpose:** Email actual expense proofs to Fandy after the conference to complete reimbursement.

| File | Type | Description | Expense category |
|------|------|-------------|------------------|
| `1_reciept.pdf` | Text PDF | Official flight receipt (e-ticket number and amount) | Transportation |
| `1_payment_record.pdf` | Screenshot | Credit card charge matching the flight | Transportation proof |
| `1_boarding_pass.pdf` | Screenshot | **Boarding pass** (airline app screenshot or scanned paper pass; must show passenger name, flight number, departure/arrival date and time) | Transportation (proof of travel) |
| `2_registration-receipt.pdf` | Text PDF | Official conference registration receipt | Registration fee |
| `2_Payment_record.pdf` | Text PDF | Credit card statement showing the registration charge | Registration proof |
| `3_hotel_receipt.pdf` | Text PDF | Hotel receipt (check-in/out dates and amount) | Accommodation |
| `3_payment_record.pdf` | Screenshot | Credit card charge matching the hotel | Accommodation proof |
| `4_acc_letter.pdf` | Text PDF | Paper abstract / supporting document (may be requested again) | Supporting doc |
| `5_first_page.pdf` | Screenshot | Acceptance notification (same type as `8_Decision.pdf`) | Supporting doc |

> **Rule of thumb:** For each reimbursable item, provide **receipt + payment record**. Card statements or app screenshots must clearly show merchant and amount.
>
> **Flights:** In addition to receipt and payment record, attach **`1_boarding_pass.pdf`** to show you actually traveled. Save screenshots from the airline app, e-boarding email, or airport kiosk after check-in; merge multi-leg passes into one PDF. The example folder does not include this file yet—add it yourself after the trip.

## Timeline and Notes

| When | Action |
|------|--------|
| After paper acceptance | Save acceptance email screenshot; export abstract |
| ≥3 weeks before trip (**in-person only**) | Prepare `material_before/` and submit **printed copies** to **Connie** (Conference Grant, Seminar Presentation, + Accommodation if needed) |
| 1 week before trip (**in-person only**) | Book a room, complete mock presentation, send invitations, archive recording |
| When registering / booking | Keep all receipts and card payment records |
| At check-in / boarding | Save boarding pass screenshot or scan (for `1_boarding_pass.pdf`) |
| After conference | Prepare `material_after/` and **email** to **Fandy** for reimbursement |

**General reminders:**

- Airfare: **2 economy quotations** for application (quotations should exceed the actual reimbursement amount); **actual ticket receipt** for reimbursement
- Registration: keep official receipt + bank/card statement
- Accommodation: IE subsidy capped at 5 nights / HK$5,000; supervisor pre-approval required
- IPL: provide CLEAR completion letter if the form asks
- HKPFS awardees: regular grants apply only after the annual travel grant is exhausted
- For screenshot PDFs, ensure sufficient resolution and no cropped critical fields
